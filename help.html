F.A.Q. for users

How does your service work?

Origen escrow is a six-step payment process that protects Buyers and Sellers in a transaction.

We holds the funds in trust while goods and / or services are delivered from Seller to the Buyer. We only releases the funds once the Buyer has received and accepted the goods and / or services.

When are funds released to the Seller?

eleases funds to the Seller when the Buyer accepts products and/or services or after the Inspection Period has expired.

How should I ship merchandise?

To enable protect all parties, We requires that all trackable and tangible merchandise be sent via a method of shipping allowing online tracking and insurance.

As the Buyer, what happens to my money when I send it to your service?

Your funds remain in our secured escrow non-interest-bearing trust account at bank until the transaction is complete. We will only release the funds once both parties are satisfied with their transaction.
